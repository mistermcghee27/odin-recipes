Green Shake

Description

This Green Shake is a nutritious flavorful snack to help you power through your day! The Green Shake is packed with vitamins and protein to help get the supplement you need without all the added calories and sugar. Plus you can swap out ingrediants to get the taste of your liking.

Ingrediants

  • Spinach
  • Almond Milk
  • Flax Seed
  • Chia Seed
  • Hemp Seed
  • Blueberries

Steps

  1. add a handful of Spinach.
  2. add 8 ounces of Almond milk>
  3. add 2 teaspoons of Flax seeds.
  4. add 2 teaspoons of Hhia seeds.
  5. add 2 teaspoons of Hemp seeds.
  6. add 1 cup of Blueberries
  7. Blend for desired consistency.
