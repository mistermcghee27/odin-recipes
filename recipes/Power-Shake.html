Chocolate Peanut Butter Protein Shake

This is my goto Protein Shake after a hard work out at the gym. It provides me with adaquete protein needed to help repair muscles after an vigorus workout. The taste also hits the spot when you have a sweet tooth but want to watch your sugar intake.

Ingredients

  • Chocoate Protein Powder
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Peanut Butter
  • Banana
  • Almond Milk
  • Ice

Steps

  1. add 4 ounces of ice.
  2. add 4 ounces of almond milk.
  3. add 1 scoop of protein powder.
  4. add 2 table spoons of cocoa.
  5. add 1 half of banana cut into slices.
  6. add 2 table spoons of peanut butter.
  7. Blend for desired consistency.
